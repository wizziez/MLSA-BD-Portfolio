About Us

Who We Are

Led by Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors, 'Student Ambassadors Bangladesh' is an emerging technical community impacting people with the aid of technology.

2001 Founded
100+ Active Ambassadors
7+ Universities

Our Mission

To empower Bangladeshi students with cutting-edge technology skills and create a vibrant tech community that drives innovation and growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading student technology community in Bangladesh, fostering the next generation of tech leaders and innovators.

Our Impact

We are comprised of community leaders, technical potentials, and tech insiders who create impacts on local, national, and global domains.
